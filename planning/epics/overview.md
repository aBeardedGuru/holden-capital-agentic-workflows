# Overview

This document decomposes the Financial Operations Automation PRD into implementable epics and stories. The work is intentionally review-first: no live accounting posting, payment initiation, or external invoice reminders are included in the MVP.

The product goal is to reduce manual finance operations work across expense tracking, invoice collection, and periodic financial reporting (weekly and month-end) while preserving auditability and human approval.

The current roadmap also includes a unification bridge epic so these finance contracts connect to a single operator-facing runtime experience instead of drifting across repos.
