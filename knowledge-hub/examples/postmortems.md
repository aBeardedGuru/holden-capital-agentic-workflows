# Postmortems

## Purpose

Store higher-signal lessons from meaningful decisions after outcomes are known.

## Entry Template

### Original Decision

What was decided.

### Assumptions That Were Right

List the assumptions that held up.

### Assumptions That Were Wrong

List the assumptions that failed.

### Early Warning Signs

What should have been noticed sooner.

### Retrieval Tags

- role relevance
- decision type
- topic

### Lesson

What the council should retrieve next time.
