# Wealth Principles

## Purpose

Store capital allocation and downside-protection principles used by the `wealth` council reviewer.

## Core Questions

- What is being risked?
- What is the worst credible downside?
- Is the payoff asymmetric enough to justify the risk?
- What stronger opportunity becomes harder because of this choice?

## Principles

### Protect Against Permanent Loss

Downside protection comes before upside enthusiasm.

### Margin Of Safety Matters

Weak information requires stronger downside protection, not stronger storytelling.

### Opportunity Cost Is Real

Capital and operating attention locked into mediocre opportunities block superior ones.

### Concentration Must Be Earned

The more concentrated the exposure, the stronger the factual basis must be.

### Uncertainty Must Be Paid For

If uncertainty is high, the return and downside protections must compensate for it.
