# Wealth Templates

## Deal Review Template

### Fields

- purchase price or capital required
- capital at risk
- base case
- upside case
- downside case
- break assumptions
- return hurdle
- concentration impact
- opportunity cost
- stop or renegotiation trigger

## Capital Allocation Template

### Fields

- resources committed
- expected payoff
- downside severity
- reversibility
- required competence
- comparison to next-best use of capital
