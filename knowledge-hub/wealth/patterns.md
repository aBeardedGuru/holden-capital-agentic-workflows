# Wealth Patterns

## Good Patterns

### Controlled Downside

The operator can clearly explain loss size, break conditions, and contingency response.

### Earned Risk

The operator understands the asset, the assumptions, and the concentration decision.

### Clear Return Hurdle

The decision is measured against explicit return and downside criteria, not excitement.

## Failure Patterns

### Story Over Math

The decision is justified by narrative while economics remain vague.

### Hidden Concentration

Multiple commitments create correlated exposure the operator is not pricing honestly.

### Maybe Means Yes

The operator proceeds despite unresolved doubts because the upside feels attractive.
