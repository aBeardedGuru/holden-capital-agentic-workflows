# Discipline Principles

## Purpose

Store execution and sustainability principles used by the `discipline` council reviewer.

## Core Questions

- Can this actually be executed well?
- What friction is being ignored?
- What important work gets displaced?
- Is the plan sustainable for months, not just days?

## Principles

### Systems Beat Willpower

If the plan depends on unusually high motivation without structural support, the plan is weak.

### Protect Deep Work

Decisions that fragment attention or erode high-leverage work carry hidden cost even when they look productive.

### Reduce Startup Friction

A good plan lowers the activation energy of the required behavior.

### Sustainability Matters

A plan that works for one sprint but creates chronic exhaustion is not operationally strong.

### Simplify Before Scaling Effort

When a plan is struggling, first reduce moving parts, handoffs, and decision fatigue before demanding more effort.
