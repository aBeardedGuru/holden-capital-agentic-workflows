# Discipline Patterns

## Good Patterns

### Scoped Commitment

The operator narrows the decision into a manageable cadence, clear owner, and simple routine.

### Protected Time Blocks

The plan explicitly reserves focused time for the work that matters most.

### Friction Removal

The operator changes environment, defaults, or workflow so the desired behavior is easier than the undesired one.

## Failure Patterns

### Motivation Dependence

The plan assumes consistent motivation instead of designing around low-energy days.

### Priority Sprawl

The operator says yes to too many concurrent priorities and loses execution quality.

### Heroic Pace

The operator confuses short bursts of effort with sustainable operating rhythm.
