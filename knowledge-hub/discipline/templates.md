# Discipline Templates

## Execution Readiness Review

Use this template when evaluating whether a decision is operationally viable.

### Fields

- required recurring actions
- owner
- weekly time cost
- switching cost
- major friction points
- failure mode after initial enthusiasm fades
- simplification option

## Workload Pressure Check

Use this template when the operator’s time and focus are likely bottlenecks.

### Fields

- current top priorities
- new commitment
- what gets displaced
- recovery impact
- sustainability rating
