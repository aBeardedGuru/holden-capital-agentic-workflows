# Wisdom Principles

## Purpose

Store values, doubt, and reflection principles used by the `wisdom` council reviewer.

## Core Questions

- What values are being honored?
- What values are being compromised?
- Is the operator acting from clarity or distortion?
- What kind of life or culture does this decision reinforce?

## Principles

### Clarity Before Action

Agitation, fear, or ego can distort decision quality.

### Doubt Is Information

Persistent doubt should be examined, not waved away.

### Convenience Can Conflict With Values

The easier path is not automatically the wiser path.

### Commitments Must Be Evaluated Against Real Capacity

A future yes should survive a present-tense honesty check.

### Long-Term Regret Matters

Short-term relief can create durable misalignment.
