# Strategy Patterns

## Good Patterns

### Focused Adjacency

The operator expands into a nearby opportunity that reinforces existing strengths.

### Deliberate Deferral

The operator says “not yet” instead of forcing a move before the system can support it.

### Explicit Trade-Off

The operator can clearly state what they are giving up to pursue the decision.

## Failure Patterns

### Opportunity Addiction

The operator accumulates adjacent opportunities until the core strategy loses coherence.

### Capability Fantasy

The operator assumes a capability gap can be overcome cheaply or informally.

### Sequence Blindness

The operator evaluates the move in isolation without considering timing and current system readiness.
