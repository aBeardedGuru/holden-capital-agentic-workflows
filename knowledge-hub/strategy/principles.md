# Strategy Principles

## Purpose

Store strategic-fit and trade-off principles used by the `strategy` council reviewer.

## Core Questions

- Does this strengthen or dilute the current strategy?
- What trade-off is actually being made?
- Is the timing right?
- Does the operator have the capability and operating model to support this?

## Principles

### Strategy Requires Trade-Offs

Every meaningful yes excludes alternatives.

### Coherence Beats Opportunism

A collection of individually attractive moves can still create a weak system.

### Capability Constraints Are Real

An opportunity that demands unsupported capabilities may be strategically premature.

### Sequencing Changes Outcomes

The same move can be smart later and reckless now.

### Good Opportunities Can Still Be Wrong

A move can be attractive in isolation and still undermine the broader direction.
